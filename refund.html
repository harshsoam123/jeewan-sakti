Refund & Cancellation

Our transparent policies regarding order returns, refunds, and cancellations.

← Back to Home Last updated: July 2026

1. Cancellation Guidelines

Orders can only be cancelled if they haven't been picked up or dispatched by our logistics partners. If you need to stop an order, drop us an urgent message over our official WhatsApp helpdesk within 12 hours of booking.

2. Return Exceptions

Because our healthcare products are highly organic Ayurvedic items, we maintain a strict policy against returning bottles that have been opened, broken, or unsealed due to consumer safety standards. We will, however, issue complete product replacements if you receive a completely mismatched package or damaged goods during courier transit.

3. Replacement Verification

To secure quick approvals for damaged shipments, customers must provide clear photos or a brief unboxing video clip to our support number within 48 hours of delivery receipt. Verified cases will be sent an automated brand new parcel container immediately.

4. Refund Timelines

Approved prepaid cancellations (UPI) will reflect refunds directly back into the customer's origin banking methods within 5 to 7 operational working days. Please note that Cash on Delivery orders are only eligible for item exchanges and replacements—no physical cash refunds can be issued.
