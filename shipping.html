Shipping & Delivery

Information regarding shipping rates, packaging timelines, and domestic delivery networks.

← Back to Home Last updated: July 2026

1. Processing & Packing Times

All successful orders verified on our network are safely packaged and dispatched within 24 to 48 operational window hours. Please keep in mind that orders are not managed or shipped on major public holidays or Sundays.

2. Estimated Delivery Windows

We distribute parcels countrywide using leading courier networks. Typical arrival windows include:

  • Metro Zones: 3 – 5 Business Working Days
  • Tier 2 & 3 Towns: 5 – 7 Business Working Days
  • North-East & Remote Locations: 7 – 10 Business Working Days

3. Package Tracking Links

Once your Ayurvedic box leaves our warehouse hub, an automated notification containing tracking links and consignment invoice codes will be immediately forwarded to your registered phone details or WhatsApp portal.

4. Incomplete or Refused Delivery

Our handling partners run up to 3 automated drop attempts before a parcel gets flagged as return-to-origin. For seamless delivery experience—especially on Cash on Delivery orders—please ensure you or an alternate person is present at the target address with accurate cash amounts.
