Privacy Policy

How Jeevan Shakti Herbs collects, uses and protects your information.

← Back to Home

Last updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Jeevan Shakti Herbs ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have, when you visit our website or place an order with us.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our website, place an order, or contact us, we may collect the following information:

  • Contact details — your name, phone number, email address and delivery address.
  • Order information — the products you order, quantity, and payment method selected (Cash on Delivery or UPI).
  • Payment confirmation — if you pay online via UPI, we may request a screenshot of your payment confirmation on WhatsApp to verify your order.
  • Communication data — messages you send us via WhatsApp, our contact form, phone or email.
  • Technical data — basic browser and device information collected automatically for website functionality (such as theme preference).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide only for purposes directly related to serving you, including to:

  • Process and deliver your orders, including coordinating with delivery partners.
  • Verify online payments made via UPI.
  • Respond to your questions, feedback or support requests.
  • Send order-related updates via WhatsApp, SMS, phone or email.
  • Improve our website, products and customer experience.

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. How We Share Your Information

We only share your information with trusted parties where necessary to fulfil your order, such as delivery/courier partners (to deliver your product) and payment processors (to verify UPI payments). These parties are only given the information required to complete their specific task.

5. WhatsApp Communication

Our order and contact forms are designed to open WhatsApp with a pre-filled message so you can confirm your request directly with our team. By using these forms, you agree to be contacted on WhatsApp regarding your order or inquiry.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you share with us. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your order and contact information only for as long as necessary to fulfil your order, respond to your inquiry, and comply with applicable legal or accounting requirements.

8. Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information, subject to any legal obligations we may have to retain it.

9. Cookies

Our website may use minimal local storage (such as remembering your dark/light theme preference) to improve your browsing experience. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

10. Children's Privacy

Our products and website are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised.
